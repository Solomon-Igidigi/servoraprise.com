Accessibility Statement — Servora Enterprise Limited™

Effective date: [Effective date]

ServoraPrise Ecosystem is committed to ensuring that our website, platforms, and digital services are accessible to all users, including people with disabilities. Our goal is to align with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, and continuously improve usability for everyone.

Accessibility Measures

To support accessibility, we implement the following practices:

  • Providing alternative text (alt text) for images and non-text content.
  • Using semantic HTML markup and logical heading structures for clarity.
  • Ensuring keyboard navigation is supported throughout the site.
  • Maintaining sufficient color contrast between text and background.
  • Offering resizable text and supporting browser accessibility features.
  • Conducting periodic accessibility reviews and testing to identify issues.

Limitations & Third-Party Content

While we strive to achieve WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliance, some limitations may remain:

  • Certain third-party widgets, plugins, or embedded content may not fully meet accessibility standards.
  • Older content or documents may not yet be fully remediated, though updates are ongoing.
  • Dynamic or real-time features may present temporary accessibility challenges.

We are committed to addressing these limitations where feasible and welcome community feedback to help us improve.

Feedback & Contact

If you encounter accessibility barriers or require assistance, please contact us:

  • Email: support@servoraprise.com
  • Subject line: Accessibility Feedback

We aim to respond to accessibility feedback within 5 business days.

Ongoing Commitment

Accessibility is an ongoing effort at Servora Enterprise Limited. We regularly review our digital platforms, apply user feedback, and explore new tools to enhance accessibility across our ecosystem.
