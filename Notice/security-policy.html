Security Policy

ServoraPrise Ecosystem takes the protection of user data, transactions, and platforms seriously. This Security Policy outlines the measures we apply to ensure system integrity and customer trust.

Our Commitments

  • Encryption of sensitive data at rest and in transit.
  • Regular risk assessments and security audits.
  • Monitoring of systems for unauthorized access.
  • Incident response plan for security breaches.
  • Compliance with PCI DSS for payment operations.
  • Staff training and access control management.

User Responsibility

Users must maintain strong, confidential passwords, enable 2FA where available, and promptly report suspicious activity to our support team.

Contact

Report security issues via security@servoraprise.com.
