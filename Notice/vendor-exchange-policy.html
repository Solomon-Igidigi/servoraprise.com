1. Service Overview

ServoraPrise Vendor Exchange provides conversion services for digital assets (cryptocurrency, gift cards, PayPal funds, airtime, electronic vouchers, and similar instruments) into Naira or other supported settlement methods. We act solely as an intermediary/agent and do not provide financial, investment, or banking advice.

2. Eligibility

You must be at least 18 years old and legally capable of entering into binding contracts. Valid identification may be required before, during, or after transactions.

3. Rates, Fees & Risks

Exchange rates and applicable fees are displayed at the point of transaction and may change without prior notice. Rates are subject to market conditions. All conversions are final once confirmed. We are not a bank — funds are not deposits and are not covered by deposit insurance. Market volatility may affect value.

4. KYC & AML Compliance

We are required to conduct Know Your Customer (KYC) and Anti-Money Laundering (AML) checks. For identity verification, we may request:

  • Government-issued ID (National ID, Driver’s License, or International Passport)
  • Proof of address (e.g., utility bill not older than 3 months)
  • A live selfie for liveness/matching
  • Evidence of source of funds

KYC is mandatory for transactions above ₦50,000, for politically exposed persons (PEPs), or where risk indicators appear. Enhanced due diligence may apply for high-value or suspicious transactions.

5. Transaction Limits & Holds

  • Small transactions may process instantly.
  • Larger or high-risk transactions may be held for verification for up to 7 business days.
  • We may suspend, delay, or refuse transactions we reasonably suspect are fraudulent or linked to money laundering or terrorism financing.

6. Settlement Time

Settlement is typically processed within 30 minutes to a few hours after successful verification. Where manual processing (e.g., bank transfer) is required, settlement may take up to 24–72 business hours, depending on banking channels or technical delays.

7. Refunds & Disputes

If a transaction fails due to our error, please notify us within 48 hours with proof of payment. We will investigate and either complete the settlement or refund in line with our Refund Policy. Disputes must follow our dispute resolution process.

8. Prohibited Uses

You may not use our Vendor Exchange services for illegal purposes, including but not limited to: money laundering, terrorist financing, fraudulent activity, sanctioned individuals/entities, or prohibited transactions. Violations will be reported to regulators and law enforcement.

9. Records & Retention

We retain transaction records and KYC/AML documentation for a minimum of 5 years, or longer where required by law. These records may be shared with regulators if legally mandated.

10. Right to Refuse or Report

We reserve the right to refuse, reverse, or block any transaction at our sole discretion. Suspicious activity may be reported to the Nigerian Financial Intelligence Unit (NFIU) or other competent authorities.

11. Limitation of Liability

To the fullest extent permitted by law, our liability is limited to the amount of the transaction in dispute. We are not responsible for indirect, incidental, or consequential losses arising from use of Vendor Exchange services.
