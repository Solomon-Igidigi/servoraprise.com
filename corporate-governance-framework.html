    • Corporate Governance Framework

    Integrity, transparency, and sustainable growth for the ServoraPrise Ecosystem.

    Executive Summary

    The ServoraPrise Ecosystem is guided by a founder-led governance model that blends visionary leadership with structured oversight. Our framework safeguards innovation, accountability, and resilience while ensuring long-term sustainability and stakeholder trust across all entities.

    Mission, Vision & Purpose

    Purpose: Empowering innovation and sustainable solutions across industries in Africa and beyond.

    Mission: Delivering excellence in aluminium fabrication, POS & digital utility services, e-commerce logistics, and tech-enabled smart systems.

    Vision: To build a resilient ecosystem where commerce, technology, and community thrive together.

    Leadership & Oversight

    Role Responsibility
    Founder / ChairpersonVisionary leadership, strategic direction, trustee liaison
    CEOCommercial operations, profitability, execution
    Innovation DirectorR&D leadership, product development, system innovation
    Community Engagement LeadPublic programs, civic partnerships, outreach
    Advisory BoardStrategic counsel, policy input, external expertise
    GTE Trustee Board (Planned)Nonprofit governance, compliance, fiduciary oversight

    Our Entities

    1. Servora Enterprise Limited

    Core operational hub, CAC Reg. July 8, 2025. Focus: Aluminium Fabrication, POS & Utility Services, Tech-Enabled Systems, E-commerce Logistics, and Diverse General Commerce.

    2. ServoraPrise by Chicent

    Founder’s solopreneur identity and innovation brand. Focus: brand development, agency services, and Recharge Republic stewardship. Established August 30, 2025.

    3. Diamore Innovation

    Established July 14, 2024. Legacy arm specializing in Aluminium Fabrication, POS Agency Services, Vendor Exchange, and General Merchandise.

    4. Recharge Republic

    Media and civic engagement arm for outreach, campaigns, and NGO partnerships.

    5. VCEE Merchant

    CAC Reg. BN: 7877673, Aug 30, 2024. Upgraded into Servora Enterprise Limited on July 8, 2025.

    6. Planned GTE

    Nonprofit trustee body for governance oversight and founder vision preservation.

    Governance Structure

    Current: Founder ➝ Management ➝ Entities ➝ Civic Arm

    Planned Expansion: GTE Oversight ➝ Founder ➝ Management ➝ Entities ➝ Civic Arm

    Key Governance Principles

    • Transparency – Open reporting and stakeholder communication
    • Accountability – Defined responsibilities with measurable outcomes
    • Integrity – Ethical standards in every operation
    • Inclusivity – Balancing commercial success with community impact
    • Resilience – Systems built to withstand market shifts
    • Founder Vision Preservation – Governance safeguards to maintain mission

    Risk & Compliance

    Centralized oversight includes operational, financial, and reputational risk reviews. The ecosystem complies with CAC, tax, labor, data privacy, and nonprofit regulations.

    Stakeholder Engagement

    • Investors: Governance transparency, strategic updates
    • Partners: Collaborative ventures, shared growth
    • Customers: Service excellence, feedback channels
    • Employees: Structured roles, skill growth, leadership opportunities
    • Community: Civic programs, media outreach, NGO collaborations
