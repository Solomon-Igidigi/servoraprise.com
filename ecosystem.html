Our Business Ecosystem

Connecting diverse services under one vision.

How We Operate

The ServoraPrise Ecosystem™ is a strategic network of business arms working together to deliver end-to-end solutions across industries. From fabrication to fintech, logistics to automation, we connect resources and expertise for maximum impact.


Our Entities

1. Servora Enterprise Limited (Core Operational Entity)

Servora Enterprise Limited stands at the center of our ecosystem, driving innovation and delivering value across multiple industries. Our expertise spans custom Aluminium Fabrication, POS & utility services, VTU operations, E-Commerce Logistics, Tech-enable system & Smart automation, and diverse general merchandise for everyday life needs. As the flagship entity, we are committed to sustainable growth, operational excellence, and solutions that make a difference.

2. ServoraPrise by Chicent (Founder’s Brand Identity — Commission Agency Division)

ServoraPrise by Chicent is the entrepreneurial identity of our founder, Vincent Chinedu Igidigi. It embodies vision, adaptability, and creativity, representing the personal leadership that shapes our ecosystem. ServoraPrise by Chicent functions as an innovation engine and agency platform, bridging opportunities, driving brand influence, and enabling value-driven partnerships across sectors. As the founder’s signature identity and umbrella brand, it ensures that every project within the ServoraPrise Ecosystem reflects the same core values of innovation, impact, and integrity.

3. Diamore Innovation (Active Legacy Project — Now Within ServoraPrise Ecosystem)

Registered on July 14, 2024, Diamore Innovation remains an active entity within the ServoraPrise Ecosystem. Founded by the same visionary behind the ecosystem, it continues to operate in its original core areas:

  • Aluminium Fabrication
  • Point of Sale (POS) Agency Services
  • Vendor Exchange Operations
  • Provision Services under General Merchandise

While Diamore Innovation has transferred its creative offspring — Recharge Republic — into the stewardship of ServoraPrise by Chicent and Servora Enterprise Limited, it maintains it's distinct role.

4. Company Limited by Guarantee (Upcoming Nonprofit Governance & Trustee Body)

A planned GTE entity that will serve as the nonprofit trustee and strategic oversight body for the entire ServoraPrise ecosystem. Its mission is to safeguard governance, uphold compliance, and ensure long-term sustainability — all while preserving the founder’s vision, principles, and ultimate control.

5. VCEE Merchant (Business Name — Upgraded to Servora Enterprise Limited)

VCEE Merchant marked the second stage of our business growth. Registered on August 30, 2024 (BN: 7877673). Initially focused on aluminium fabrication, it expanded into a multi-sector operation. On July 8, 2025, it was formally upgraded and incorporated as Servora Enterprise Limited. The legacy of VCEE Merchant remains a strong foundation for our present and future operations.
