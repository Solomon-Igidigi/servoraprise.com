    About Recharge Republic

    Recharge Republic is the Civic & Media Engagement Arm of the ServoraPrise Ecosystem, proudly operating under Servora Enterprise Limited and ServoraPrise by Chicent. It was born from the innovation legacy of Diamore Innovation, with a mission to inspire, inform, and involve communities through meaningful digital and offline engagement.

    From civic campaigns to storytelling initiatives, Recharge Republic connects with people on issues that matter — using the power of media, technology, and creativity to foster awareness, unity, and positive change.
